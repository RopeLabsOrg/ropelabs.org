# Donate to RopeLabs

Your donations help us continue providing rope workshops, educational resources, and safe spaces for the rope community at events like Congress, EMF, Bornhack, and other hacker camps.

## How to Donate

You can donate in the following ways:

- **Cash** in our donation box at events and assemblies
- **Bank transfer**: IE26SUMU99036512523634 (Name: Peg Productions)
- **[Online via SumUp](https://pay.sumup.com/b2c/Q3LMF1GC)** (3% fee)
- **Card payment** at events and assemblies (3% fee)

## Where Your Money Goes

All donations go to the RopeLabs organization to fund future events and activities, not to individuals.

Your contributions help us:

- Purchase and maintain shared equipment (rope, safety gear, mats, etc.)
- Cover costs for workshops and educational materials
- Support assembly and village spaces at events
- Provide practice rope and safety equipment on a pay-what-you-want basis
- Build and maintain our community resources

We operate on a not-for-profit basis, and all funds are used to support the RopeLabs community and our mission of creating safe, accessible spaces for rope education and practice.
